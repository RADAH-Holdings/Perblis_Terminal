## The system in one paragraph

Terminal looks like the best-run yard in Lagos: **safety amber on near-black ink**, squared and structural, with monospaced numerals doing the talking. It is *not* austere — warmth comes from the warm paper field, generous photography, the hazard-stripe and manifest motifs used as craft details, big confident numerals, and motion that behaves like good machinery: short, precise, damped. Trust artifacts (badges, locked terms, timelines, receipts) are the most designed objects in the product.

## Principles (gate every decision)

1. **Legible at noon.** Outdoor sunlight is the reference environment. Contrast budgets are non-negotiable.
2. **Sturdy, not soft.** Structure shows: borders, rules, columns. Nothing floats vaguely.
3. **Numbers are heroes.** If a screen has a figure on it, the figure is the best-set thing on the screen.
4. **Trust is rendered.** Verification, locked terms, records, and receipts get first-class, signature treatments.
5. **Color is information.** Two brand inks + class hues + status hues. Decoration never borrows a meaning-carrying color.
6. **One system, two postures.** The portal is dense and operational; the app is focused and glanceable. Same tokens, different rhythm — never different identities.
7. **Appealing through craft, not effects.** No gradients-for-mood, no glassmorphism. Beauty = composition, type, spacing, photography, and four signature motifs used with restraint.
